Sprout: Glucose Wearable Product Exploration

Project Information

  • Product exploration for a health monitoring system designed to help parents with kids with diabetes gain a deeper insight into their child's health so they can make informed decisions.
  • Worked on a cross functional team of engineers to explore this product market and create a potential prototype
  • Conducted a market analysis and competition matrix to determine the customer needs and narrow down the scope of our solution
  • Designed an interactive Figma prototype to simulate what the user experience might look like.
