Garmin: Product Management Internship

Project Information

  • Undertook 5 new features for the Clipboard Web App to enhance user experience for over 60% of our coaches. Utilized technologies like React, Electron, Figma, and Garmin Health and Activity APIs.
  • Identified a potential $4 million new market opportunity for Garmin Golf Coaching. Conducted user interviews and surveys with over 30 coaches, validated product-market fit, and designed an interactive Figma prototype to visualize golf workflows and new features.
  • Grew Garmin Clipboard by 8,500 athletes, resulting in more than $40,000 in Garmin Device sales. Showcasing Clipboard’s value to coaches through customer interviews and relayed feedback to design, engineering, and product teams to improve the app.
